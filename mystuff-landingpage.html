Key Features

Simplify insurance claims with AI-powered tools

Don't tell your insurance providers, but their insurance claims are currently outdated. AND, they can be tedious and emotional. MyStuff automates your insurance process by storing the home inside a digital archive, providing you with easy access to your home from anywhere.

Automates your claims with AI integration

  • Stores your specific information needed to file insurance claims
  • Pre-existing templates for different insurance claims
  • Exportable CSV files for seamless documentation

Documents your belongings with a click

  • MyStuff documents your belongings, valuables, and furniture with just a snap using Computer Vision Segmentation
  • Categorize, identify, and value items automatically

Matches you with optimal resources

  • Integrates AI to help you with online/in-person insurance adjusters for precise value assessments
  • Allows for adjuster ratings and crowdsourced opinions
  • Suggests messages and message templates for communication

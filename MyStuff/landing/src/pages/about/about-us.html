About MyStuff

Empowering homeowners with smart inventory management since 2024

Our Story

MyStuff was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to give homeowners peace of mind by helping them manage their belongings intelligently and effortlessly.

What started as a solution for documenting home inventory for insurance purposes has evolved into a comprehensive platform that helps thousands of homeowners organize, track, and protect their valuable possessions.
